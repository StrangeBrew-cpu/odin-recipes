Lemon Bars

Sweet treats for a special gal

Tasty lemon bars hit the spot on a hot summer day. Bake these and put them on a tray, they won't last long. Even the crumbs are delicious. Some might even want to lick the plate!

Ingredients

Shortbread Crust

  • 1 cup Gluten-Free Bake Mix 2, OR Keto Bake Mix
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • Liquid sweetener to equal 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract

Lemon Filling

  • 6 large eggs
  • Liquid sweetener to equal 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup powdered sweetener
  • 1/2 cup Gluten-Free Bake Mix 2, OR Keto Bake Mix
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest, finely chopped

Steps

Prehead oven to 350 degrees

Shortbread Crust

  1. In medium bowl, combine Gluten-Free Bake Mix 2, and almond flour.
  2. In small bowl, combine melted butter, liquid sweetener, and almond extract.
  3. Add to dry ingredients and mix well.
  4. Press into a 8 x 11-inch dish or a 9 x 9-inch dish.
  5. Bake 10 minutes.
  6. Set aside to cool.

Lemon Filling

  1. In mixer with whipping assembly, whip eggs together with liquid sweetener.
  2. Add powdered sweetener and lemon juice; whip.
  3. Add bake mix, OR alternative, and lemon zest and whip just until nicely combined.
  4. Pour lemon curd over crust.
  5. Bake 15 to 20 minutes until softly set.
  6. Let cool completely and dust with powdered sweetener, if desired.
